Solutions!

Components are designed to work seamlessly together

Raspberry Pi 5

The brain of the device. It controls all processes, from image capture to running the AI model and displaying results.

Camera Module

Captures clear images of the eggs for analysis using the AI model.

Touchscreen Display

Shows fertility results in real time for ease of monitoring.

LED Lamps

The light source for candling. These illuminate the egg to make internal features visible for accurate scanning.
